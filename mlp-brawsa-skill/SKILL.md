# BRAWSA Prompt Builder

## Overview

BRAWSA is a six-component framework for designing precision AI prompts. Most people hand AI a document and hope it figures out what matters. That's chance. BRAWSA turns that into choice—by encoding your analytical judgment, your specific purpose, and your exact standards into a reusable prompt system.

BRAWSA stands for:
- **Background**: Context the AI needs before touching the document
- **Role**: What is AI's job on this specific task
- **Assumptions**: What should AI take as given and what it should not assume
- **Workflow**: Exactly how AI should do the work
- **Success**: What a good output looks like—specifically
- **Audience**: Who is this output ultimately for

## When to Use This Skill

Use BRAWSA when:
- A user has a specific analytical task
- They have a clear purpose and success criteria
- They want to move beyond generic summaries
- They want a prompt that encodes their expertise
- They need consistent, predictable outputs
- The task involves reviewing, analyzing, or synthesizing documents

## Key Principles

1. **Specificity is Power** — More specific = more precise output
2. **Workflow is Where Your Judgment Lives** — Encode how you think
3. **Success Must Be Measurable** — Define what "good" looks like
4. **Assumptions Prevent Gaps** — Tell AI which gaps NOT to fill
5. **Audience Shapes Everything** — Different readers need different formats

## Quick Start

1. Understand the analytical task
2. Define each of the six components
3. Build your prompt
4. Test on a real task
5. Refine and reuse for similar tasks

## The Six Components

**Background** (2–4 sentences) — Context the AI needs

**Role** (2–3 sentences) — AI's specific job on this task

**Assumptions** (4–6 items) — What to take as given

**Workflow** (Detailed steps) — Exactly how to do the work

**Success** (Specific description) — What good output looks like

**Audience** (2–3 sentences) — Who will read this and why

## Philosophy

**Chance vs. Choice:**
- Chance: Paste document, type "summarize this," hope for the best
- Choice: Engineer a prompt that tells AI exactly what to look for, how to analyze it, and what "good" means
